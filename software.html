EMORPH2- Tools for Evolutionary morphology and morphometrics.

Tools to investigate the evolution of complex traits at both the micro and macroevolutionary scale.

PCMKappa- Phylogenetic comparative kappa models.

Add-on package to PCMBase and PCMfit with kappa models- proportional models used to model the rate of evolution. Used to incorporate microevolutionary assumptions into macroevolutionary modeling.

EVOLQG- Tools for Evolutionary Quantitative Genetics.

Package for evolutionary quantitative genetics, described in Melo D, Garcia G, Hubbe A et al. EvolQG - An R package for evolutionary quantitative genetics [version 1; referees: 1 approved, 1 approved with reservations] F1000Research 2015, 4:925 (doi: 10.12688/f1000research.7082.1)
